LULC change Detection and Prediction using CA-Markov

The project conducted a land use and land cover (LUCC) change analysis for Banepa Valley from 1990 to 2021 using supervised classification techniques and predicted future changes up to 2040 using the CA-Markov model. The study revealed significant urban expansion, forest degradation, and agricultural land fluctuations, with accuracy assessments showing substantial to almost perfect agreement. The findings underscore the impact of population growth and urbanization on land cover, providing valuable insights for land management and urban planning in the region.
