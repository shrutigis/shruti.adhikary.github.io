Flood Risk Assessment for Residential Areas along Kuils River, Cape Town

Project Overview

Flooding is a serious concern for the residents along the Kuils River. To address this, I used HEC-RAS simulation software to model various flood scenarios. This helped me identify areas at high risk of flooding and propose effective mitigation measures.

Data Collection and Processing

I started by gathering high-resolution photogrammetry data, which included Digital Elevation Models (DEM) and point cloud data. This data provided a detailed view of the area's topography and was crucial for building an accurate hydraulic model in HEC-RAS.

Developing the Hydraulic Model

Using the collected data, I developed a detailed hydraulic model in HEC-RAS. This model simulated different flood scenarios, showing how water would flow during heavy rains and where it would likely overflow its banks.

Analyzing Flood Scenarios

The simulations revealed the potential inundation extents for various flood events. By analyzing these scenarios, I identified high-risk zones where flooding could cause significant damage to homes and infrastructure.
