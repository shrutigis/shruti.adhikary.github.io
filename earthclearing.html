Earth Clearing Monitoring using QGIS and Sentinel Satellite Imagery

Project Overview

As a dedicated Earth Clearing Monitoring Specialist, I have been working on a project in Wyoming, USA, focusing on analyzing Sentinel satellite imagery to track and document earth clearing activities. My role involves utilizing advanced GIS tools and techniques to ensure precise monitoring and reporting.

Key Responsibilities

  • QGIS Analysis:Leveraged QGIS to thoroughly analyze Sentinel satellite imagery, providing accurate insights into earth clearing activities.
  • Documentation and Identification: Systematically identified and documented instances of earth clearing, linking them to designated point features for precise record-keeping.
  • Geojson Management: Regularly updated geojson files with relevant fields, ensuring accurate records and seamless data sharing for stakeholders.
